Take a look at what's in front of you, tear a strip of paper off the board, move that board to the left and turn it upside down. Use what's here to make something, anything. You can change the background color of this projection using the color picker in the top left corner. You also have the option to add a layer of digital collage by using the image assets on the slides to collage on a new slide.

By returning to the level of abstract making, experimentation and curiosity we once had as children, Esc is an experiment in itself. Setting aside the institutionalised prescribed method of having a clear goal, Esc challenges the premise that there has to be meaning in the design process. Meaning can come after making.

HAVE FUN, MAKE, ESCAPE.
